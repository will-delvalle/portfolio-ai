Case 01 / Tier-1 LLM Provider

Audio and Video Safety

Challenge

The team needed to find severe policy violations in the model using audio and video files in realistic ways, avoiding obvious testing patterns.

Solution

I created a practical workflow based on the client's rules. I standardized file organization, set up a fast support channel for questions, and managed the volume to avoid team overload.

Impact

Better file organization improved our audits. We reduced rework and maintained a steady delivery of consistent, well-documented tasks.

Case 02 / Global AI Research Lab

Deterministic Autonomous Agents

Challenge

The client needed data science tasks with very specific difficulty levels. The results had to be verified by code, not by a human reviewer's opinion.

Solution

I worked to ensure data accuracy across creation and review platforms. I updated tracking versions, monitored failure rates, and raised technical issues to leadership.

Impact

System tracking errors decreased. We kept the project within the required difficulty range, ensuring specialists were paid without delays.

Case 03 / Enterprise AI Solutions

Legal Compliance Operations

Challenge

The team had to create examples for a legal AI project using restricted platforms. Doubts about technical and legal limitations were blocking progress.

Solution

I mapped over 150 process types to identify exactly where we were blocked. I organized expert recruitment and centralized communication to keep the team focused while waiting for approvals.

Impact

Clear visibility of blockages helped leadership make fast decisions. We prepared the examples exactly as requested and structured the path to scale production.

Case 04 / Tier-1 LLM Provider

Language Quality Control

Challenge

A Portuguese language project suffered from poor quality. A third-party review team was applying incorrect grammar rules, causing disputes and putting the contract at risk.

Solution

I investigated the root cause and sought official validation from the Brazilian Academy of Letters. I documented the errors with clear evidence and presented a direct report to leadership.

Impact

The client removed the underperforming vendor. The Portuguese pipeline quality recovered quickly, trust was restored, and operations scaled safely.
